H3xt0 Help

  1. Enter **Income** and **Essentials**. You can use the new Income by Pay and Essentials (Itemized) cards below to normalize and auto-fill totals.
  2. Add **Debts**: name, balance, APR %, term (months), min payment. H3xt0 recommends where extra dollars go.
  3. Set a **Goal date** and move the **What-If** slider to see cash-flow and payoff curves update.
  4. Optional: generate **Payday reminders** (.ics) to nudge avalanche payments on each payday.
Tip: long-press any input for a short tip.

Use Income by Pay Schedule to enter per-paycheck net and choose frequency: Monthly=12, Bi-weekly=26, Twice-monthly=24. The monthly total updates automatically. Click Copy monthly total to auto-fill your main Income field.

Add rent, utilities, subscriptions, etc. Seasonal changes? Just edit the rows. Click Copy monthly total to auto-fill your main Essentials field.

Enter debts. H3xt0 uses an APR-aware avalanche (pay highest APR first) while honoring your goal date. Extra dollars are suggested in Plan Summary.

Set your target purchase date. Use the What-If slider to see cash-flow and payoff curves respond instantly.

Pick your pay frequency and next payday, then download the **.ics** file. Import to your phone’s calendar for reliable reminders that work offline on Android & iPhone.

Private by design. H3xt0 stores everything on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no ads.
